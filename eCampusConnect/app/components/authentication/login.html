WHY eCampusConnect

eCampusConnect is initiating with a campus connect program i.e. eCampusConnect Campus Connect to broaden the scope for students to get familiar with the practicality of industries; majorly emphasizing on building a strong foundation required in IT industries. It will basically connect students to the industry.

In this program, a major glimpse of our routine work, future requirements, technologies used etc are updated on the website from where students could get to know about the industrial requisitions and they may update themselves accordingly.

Also, Some of the sample practice test papers are made available to the students on the website from where they can prepare themselves for the recruitment process of eCampusConnect.
